About Us

ONE STOP SHOP ENGINEERING CONTRACTORS – SERVICE PROVIDERS


OVERVIEW

We TRITECH Engineers (Pvt) Ltd established in 1999 focusing to become one of the best engineering contractor companies in Sri Lanka.

We are proud to say that TRITECH are in the 18th successive year, serving the country, being achieved our target to be one of the leading Engineering service providers in Sri Lanka.

Under the umbrella of "TRITECH Group of Companies", has gained prominence in achieving high-quality solutions for industrial requirements in Civil, Mechanical, Plumbing & Environmental fields.

We have valued the "appreciation" of our customers – of many – and diversified our activities into two more companies namely, Tritech Marketing Int. Pvt. Ltd, a Trading company providing Engineering products and solutions. We have extended our boundaries to overseas as well with Maldivian Tritech Pvt Ltd.

Our Vision And Mission

Our Vision:

Sri Lanka's choice of customers in construction industry by serving as one stop shop, best in quality, cost leadership and timely delivery, in civil, mechanical, electrical and plumbing engineering combinations.

Our Mission:

Continuous Improvement in quality, safety and reliability standards in the services offered.
Cost Leadership through optimizing the profitability of the resources managed.
