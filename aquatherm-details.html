AQUATHERM POLYPROPYLENE PIPE SYSTEMS

Aquatherm's journey began in 1973, founded by Gerhard Rosenberg to produce warm water underfloor heating systems. Starting in a garage and basement, the company has grown to become a global leader in polypropylene pipe systems for plant engineering and building services.

Aquatherm Green Pipe

Revolutionizing the plastic piping industry with technical reliability worldwide

Aquatherm Green Pipe has revolutionized the plastic piping industry, proving its technical reliability worldwide for decades. Aquatherm Blue Pipe

Designed for efficient heating and cooling distribution in closed systems

Aquatherm Blue Pipe is designed for efficient heating and cooling distribution in closed systems, as well as a range of industrial applications.
